Antigen Discovery

Description

Prioritizing antigens for cancer immunotherapy is now possible through advanced bioinformatics tools. We integrate multimodal data to discover tumor-associated and neoantigens across patient data. By analyzing mRNA expression profiles, binding affinity, and immunogenicity, we apply sophisticated machine learning to derive a ranking system enabling the identification of high-priority targets. Furthermore, leveraging TCRpMHC structural data, our pipeline can uncover cross-reactive antigens, filtering candidates based on off-target toxicity risk. Boost your research with Lexanomics' expertise and stay ahead in the quest for innovative cancer therapies. We provide:

  • Curated panel of cancer-related antigens (neoantigens and tumor-associated antigens) for targeted investigation, streamlining the discovery process.
  • Comprehensive mRNA expression data across pan-cancer datasets (21 TCGA datasets and more), offering a broad scope of insights into antigen expression patterns.
  • Structural models made with cutting-edge techniques, reporting precise TCRpMHC interactions, enhancing the understanding of antigen recognition.
  • T-cell cross-reactivity predictions based on physicochemical properties across eluted peptides from multiple immunopeptidomics datasets, reducing the risk of potential off-target toxicity.
